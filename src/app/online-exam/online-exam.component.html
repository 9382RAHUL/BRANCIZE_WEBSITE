Working Principle of Online Examinations

Computer-Based Tests (CBT)

Computer-based tests are conducted on a computer, typically at designated test centers. These exams use specialized software to deliver questions and capture responses, ensuring a controlled and secure environment. Test-takers benefit from a streamlined process, as CBTs often feature user-friendly interfaces and automated features like timers and navigation tools.

Internet-Based Tests (IBT)

Internet-based tests are administered over the internet, allowing test-takers to complete their exams from any location with a reliable internet connection. This method offers maximum flexibility, as individuals can use their own devices, such as computers, tablets, or smartphones. IBTs provide the ultimate convenience, enabling test-takers to choose a comfortable and familiar setting, and often allowing for scheduling at a time that suits them best.
