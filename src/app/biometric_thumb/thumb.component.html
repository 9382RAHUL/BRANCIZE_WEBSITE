About Thumb-Based Biometrics

Thumb-based biometrics uses unique thumbprint patterns for identity verification. It's widely used for unlocking devices, accessing secure areas, and authenticating transactions, offering high accuracy and security. This technology ensures only authorized users gain access to sensitive information or locations.
