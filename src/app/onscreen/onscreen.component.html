Working Principle of OSMS

Candidates can effortlessly upload their exam scripts onto our secure digital platform, ensuring they are safely archived. Our system assigns scripts to markers based on their expertise and schedule. Markers utilize advanced digital tools to grade, annotate, and provide detailed feedback on their preferred devices. Quality supervisors oversee the evaluation process to ensure consistency and accuracy, with the authority to refine marks when necessary. Results are efficiently aggregated by our Online Script Management System (OSMS) for secure distribution to stakeholders. Our software provides comprehensive evaluation metrics, enabling continuous improvement in assessment methodologies.
